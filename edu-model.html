Mathematical Definition

The system uses a system of coupled equations to determine the output. Because resources are finite, these variables act as constraints on one another.

$$ \begin{align} \text{Recruitment:} & \quad N_{in} = N_{max} \cdot (1 - A^{1.5}) \\ \text{Final Knowledge:} & \quad K = (\theta_{base} + \alpha A) + (\gamma R) \\ \text{Retention Rate:} & \quad r = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-5(K - S)}} \\ \text{Outcomes:} & \quad \text{Graduates} = N_{in} \cdot r \end{align} $$
